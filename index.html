I am a Year 4 Phd student supervised by Prof. Guodong Li at the Department of Statistics and Actuarial Science. I received my Bachelor in Yuanpei College from Peking University in 2021, where I was interested in deep learning research.

My research focuses on deep learning, integral equation, computer vision, time series forecasting and sequential modeling. I have published some papers in machine learning conferences, including the ICLR and ICML. My recent work mainly considers the sequential researches including sequential data and sequential model. I also serve as the reviewer of ICML and NIPs.

At Peking University, I chose the Data Science and Big Data Technology Program and studied with Prof. Zhi Geng in the School of Mathematical Science. Form 2021 to 2022, I study with Dr. Zhonghua Liu about the solution with deep learning method for integral equation in statistical problems at the Department of Biostatistics in Columbia University. Then I studied with Prof. Guodong Li about time series forecasting and transfer my study field into sequential researches.

Also I give the tutorial of two courses, Probability and Statistics and Advanced Statistical Inference.
